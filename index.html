About

- Shawn Mendes

Shawn Peter Raul Mendes is a Canadian singer and songwriter. He was born on August 8, 1998. He gained a following in 2013, posting song covers on the video-sharing application Vine.

In his album, he has written many songs. Such as Stitches, Treat You Better until the latest one, It'll be Okay. He's a big fan of Chocolate Muffin. And last, He gets a lot of support from his fans called Mendes Army ❤️
