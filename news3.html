2022 Oscars: Shawn Mendes As Presenter for The 2022 Oscars

By: Melati Alya Puti Irawan | June 6, 2022

Shawn Mendes, a singer, debuted a new look at the 2022 Oscars. Shawn Mendes was scheduled to be one of the event's presenters.

Shawn Mendes was set to be one of the Oscar presenters in 2022. According to The Hollywood Reporter, Mendes appeared on the red carpet for the first time on Monday (28/3/2022) in a classic tuxedo and bow tie designed by Dolce & Gabbana.

A black double-breasted tuxedo is worn with a white shirt and contrasting black buttons.

"We wanted to honor the occasion with a truly classic and iconic look," said Shawn Mendes' stylist Tiffany Briseno. Tiffany Briseno also mentioned that Shawn Mendes' height made it look more elegant. "Overall it looks very luxurious and classic," he added.

Briseno admitted that he only had about two weeks to provide the clothes after Mendes was confirmed as a presenter at the Oscars. "We know this is Shawn's first Oscar, so it must be something special." "He fell in love with the idea of a double-breasted suit, so we went right to work on it." "We've also previously collaborated with Dolce & Gabbana," he added.

Mendes completed the look with rings and watches from his personal collection. Shawn Mendes is nervous as he prepares for the 2022 OSCARS event, so he practices meditation to calm himself down. You can watch it below on YouTube Vanity Fair.
